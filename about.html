About BioSphere

BioSphere is a pioneering research and education hub dedicated to transforming healthcare through cutting-edge research, advanced technology, and innovative learning.

  • Mission: Our mission is to bridge the gap between biotechnology and digital innovation, driving scientific discoveries that shape the future of medicine.
  • Vision: To establish BioSphere as a leading research hub driving breakthrough innovations in cancer diagnostics, biotechnology, and healthcare solutions.
  • Innovative Solutions: From AI-driven diagnostics to precision medicine, we develop transformative solutions that push the boundaries of biotechnology. By fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing, we empower the next generation of scientists to drive impactful change in global healthcare.

Founder's Message


We envisioned BioSphere as more than just a startup — it's a movement towards transforming the landscape of research, diagnostics, and innovation, especially in regions that are often overlooked, like Bihar. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: quality research and impactful learning should not be limited by geography.

At BioSphere, we are committed to advancing affordable and accessible solutions in cancer diagnostics, biotechnology, and healthcare innovation. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application by fostering a hands-on learning environment for aspiring scientists, students, and professionals.

We are proud to empower young minds through research-oriented projects, skill-building workshops, and collaborative opportunities that nurture curiosity and creativity. Our focus areas — from oncolytic therapy and genetic engineering to AI/ML in health and plant-based therapies — reflect our drive to create real-world solutions that make a difference.

We invite you to join us in our journey. Whether you’re a student, researcher, or industry expert, BioSphere is a platform where ideas grow, collaborations thrive, and innovation becomes reality.

Together, let’s create a future where science knows no boundaries.

With warm regards,
